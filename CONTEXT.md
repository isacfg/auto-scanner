# Auto Scanner Domain Glossary

## Photograph

A single physical printed photograph being digitized. A Photograph always has a Front and may have a Back.

## Front

The primary image-bearing side of a Photograph. Capturing the Front completes a Photograph when Back Capture is disabled.

## Back

The optional reverse side of a Photograph. It belongs to the same Photograph as its Front and is not sent for AI restoration by default.

## Capture Session

A continuous period in which a user digitizes multiple Photographs on the iPhone. Back Capture is configured for the session and can be changed for a Photograph that has not started capture.

## Supported Capture

A Capture Session performed with the iPhone stationary in a stand above the Photograph. This is the recommended capture mode.

## Handheld Capture

A Capture Session performed while the user holds the iPhone. It is a supported MVP mode, with capture readiness evaluated independently from Supported Capture.

## Back Capture

An optional Capture Session mode. When enabled, the app captures the Front, asks the user to turn over the same Photograph, captures the Back, and links both sides. It is disabled by default.

## Original

The immutable camera output for one side of a Photograph. It is never overwritten by cropping, corrections, or AI processing.

## Master

A non-AI-derived version of an Original with accepted geometric corrections such as crop, rotation, and perspective correction.

## Restoration

An AI-produced derivative of a Front Master. A Restoration never replaces its Original or Master.

## Library

The user's collection of Photographs, their sides, derived assets, metadata, and processing state shared between the iPhone and Mac apps.
